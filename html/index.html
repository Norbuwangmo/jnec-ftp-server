About

Learn more about the JNEC Digital Repository and its purpose

About the Repository

The JNEC Digital Repository is a centralized academic platform developed to store, organize, and share digital learning resources for students and faculty. It provides easy access to student projects, question papers, software tools, and academic materials through a clean and user-friendly web interface.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support teaching, learning, and research at Jigme Namgyel Engineering College by creating a reliable digital repository where students and lecturers can access academic resources anytime. The system helps preserve student works and promotes knowledge sharing across departments.

How it Works

1
Students log in and upload their final year projects and academic files through the web interface.
2
The files are stored securely on the backend FTP server developed from scratch in C.
3
Users can browse projects, question papers, and software resources by category.
4
Tutors or administrators can review and approve uploaded content before making it publicly available.
5
Approved resources remain accessible for future students and faculty members.

Key Features

Organized Resources
Projects, papers, and software arranged by category and department
Easy Upload
Students can upload academic materials through the portal
Quick Download
Users can easily access and download approved resources
Secure Access
Managed content review and controlled visibility of materials

Who Can Use This Portal?

This repository is designed for JNEC students, lecturers, tutors, and administrators. Students can use it to find past question papers and reference materials, while faculty members can use it to review submissions and support academic resource management.
